Capture new growth opportunities through FWA with 5G NR

Today’s subscribers expect robust bandwidth and low latency for the latest services and leading-edge applications. However, in some suburban or rural areas, broadband connections many households or businesses are either underserved or performance-limited. The Zyxel Fixed Wireless Access (FWA) solution allows operators to leverage 5G technology to deliver high-capacity, premium broadband services featuring high-speed connectivity, enhanced network security and deployment flexibility. Zyxel’s flexible 5G FWA portfolio enhances and expands mobile broadband deployment options, generating new opportunities within existing and future markets.

Solution Benefits

Lower latency, more premium service opportunities

The latency of 5G NR is an estimated 60 to 120 times lower than 4G, which means the delay from when data is uploaded to when it reaches its target is nearly undetectable. In fact, the ultra-low latency of 5G NR could eventually be less than 10 milliseconds, making it a perfect for providing premium, timing-sensitive services such as gaming and cloud applications, live video streaming, and VR/AR.

Wide spectrum bandwidth, higher network efficiency

By opening new ranges of the spectrum, 5G NR creates additional opportunities for FWA deployments, delivering the necessary bandwidth to support high-definition applications. 5G NR’s increase in bandwidth accelerates Internet speeds and reduces network latency for an overall improvement in service of 80%.

Wide product portfolio for flexible deployments

The future is here. Zyxel offers a wide product portfolio featuring different levels of 5G NR devices — outdoor, indoor and portable — suitable for a range of application scenarios and a variety of network infrastructures. With Zyxel 5G NR solutions, you can expand your FWA service offerings in any direction, reaching your target markets with ease.

SA/NSA dual-mode compliant to secure your CAPEX

To secure your investments in 5G NR, Zyxel 5G NR FWA solutions are interoperable with both Standalone (SA) and Non-Standalone (NSA) deployments. In addition to deployment flexibility, Zyxel 5G NR routers support new bands and reframing bands. This enhances the connection quality and provides your subscribers with an excellent connectivity experience.

5G NR solution delivers Mesh WiFi coverage for the entire home

To offer your subscribers ultra-fast, whole-home WiFi coverage, Zyxel incorporates today’s hottest mesh technologies in its 5G NR FWA solutions.

The solution consists of mounting a flexible Outdoor Unit (ODU), Indoor Gateway and EasyMesh standards-compliant WiFi repeater. Together, this provides a superior self-adaptive, zero-dead-zones superior home WiFi experience.

5G NR Scenarios

Outdoor deployment

Outdoor deployment

In the areas with weak signal coverage, an ODU is deployed outdoors and connected with Indoor Mesh Gateway via a long power over Ethernet (PoE) cable.

Indoor deployment

Indoor deployment

In areas with strong signal coverage, the ODU is integrated with the Indoor Mesh Gateway via a short PoE cable and deployed inside the building.

Indoor deployment with
basement facility

Indoor deployment with basement facility

For basement deployments covering WiFi dead spots, the ODU is deployed on the first floor and connected to the Indoor Mesh Gateway located in the basement via a long PoE cable.

Zyxel LTE Ally app optimizes user experience

Zyxel 5G NR solutions come with the Zyxel LTE Ally app, a simple and intuitive app allowing your subscribers to set up the device with only a few taps.

The Zyxel LTE Ally app eliminates the need to purchase an additional configuration app for your subscribers and reduces service calls during the device installation process.
